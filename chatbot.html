How does the chatbot work?
It's trained on a custom knowledge base built around your business. When a visitor asks a question, it matches their input against that knowledge base and responds accurately. It only knows what you've provided — it doesn't improvise or make things up.
How it works

What makes it different.

Knowledge
Trained on your data
We build a custom knowledge base from the information you provide — your services, pricing, availability, booking process, FAQs, and policies. The chatbot only answers based on what you've given us, which means it answers accurately and doesn't guess or fabricate information.
Language
Multilingual by default
The chatbot responds in the language the visitor is using. The knowledge base is built in each language separately to ensure responses are natural and accurate, not machine-translated. Works in EN, FR, DE, NL, ES, and any other language you need.
Matching
Handles typos and informal language
Visitors rarely type perfectly. The chatbot uses fuzzy matching to understand variations in spelling, informal phrasing, and abbreviated questions. A visitor typing "whats ur price" gets the same accurate response as one typing "What is your pricing?"
Leads
Collects contact details
When a visitor's question suggests they're interested in booking or getting a quote, the chatbot can prompt them to leave their email or phone number. Those details are collected and stored for you to follow up.
Hosting
Self-hosted, no platform fees
The chatbot runs directly on your website's infrastructure. There are no third-party platform subscriptions, no per-message fees, and no external dependencies that could go offline or change their pricing. You own it outright once it's built.
Option
API-based alternative
For use cases that need more open-ended or complex responses, an API-based version is available. This connects to an external AI service and handles a wider range of questions. Usage costs are billed at the provider's rate plus a management margin. We'll advise which option fits your use case better during the brief.
