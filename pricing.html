FAQ

Common questions.

Anything not covered — ask us directly.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
No. Setup fees are one-time — pay once, done. Monthly services (chatbot hosting, phone agent usage, campaign management) run month-to-month. You can stop at any time with no penalties.
What if I want multiple services?
Each service is priced and managed independently. You can start with one and add others later. There's no bundle requirement — mix and match exactly what your business needs.
How does monthly billing work?
Chatbot hosting is $49/mo, invoiced at the start of each month. Phone agent usage is billed at end of month based on actual call volume. Campaign management is invoiced monthly at the agreed rate. Ad spend goes directly from your card to the ad platform — we never handle it.
Can I see examples before committing?
Yes. Our portfolio shows past website and AI product work. The Creatives page has a live example of an animated ad. For campaigns, we can walk you through a previous campaign structure on a call.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
Every project includes revision rounds — we don't mark it complete until the agreed scope is delivered to your satisfaction. If something isn't right, we fix it as part of the project, not as an extra charge.
Do you work with businesses outside the US?
Yes. All work is fully remote. We've built for clients in France, Luxembourg, the US, and more. Invoicing is in USD. Typical response within 24 hours, regardless of time zone.
