Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 2026

If you have connected your Facebook or Instagram account to a Zoomy service and would like to request deletion of your data, you can do so at any time. This page explains how.

What data we may hold

When you interact with Zoomy through Facebook or Instagram — for example by commenting on a post or sending a message — we may temporarily store your user ID, message content, and interaction timestamps for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. We do not sell this data or share it with third parties.

How to request data deletion

  1. Send an email to contact@zoomy.services with the subject line Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include your name and the Facebook or Instagram account associated with the data you want deleted.
  3. We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.
You can also disconnect Zoomy from your Facebook account directly through Facebook's settings: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites, then remove Zoomy from the list. This immediately revokes our access to your account.

Questions

If you have any questions about your data or this process, contact us at contact@zoomy.services.
