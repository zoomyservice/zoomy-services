TikTok Integration

Connect your TikTok account to let Zoomy automatically publish your marketing videos — saving time and keeping your content consistent.

Connect your TikTok account

Zoomy uses TikTok's official Login Kit to securely connect your account. We will request the following permissions:

  • 👤
    user.info.basic
    Your TikTok display name and profile picture — so Zoomy can confirm which account is connected.
  • 📤
    video.upload
    Upload video files to TikTok on your behalf, ready for publishing.
  • 🚀
    video.publish
    Directly post approved videos to your TikTok profile using Content Posting API.
