Process

How we build your agent.

build-agent.sh
$ ./build-phone-agent --client
Step 1: Business briefing & knowledge extraction
→ Services, pricing, hours, FAQs
→ Tone & personality guidelines
Step 2: Calendar & tool integration
→ Connect availability system
→ Configure booking webhooks
Step 3: Voice & language configuration
→ Voice selection & tuning

Built around your business

The agent is trained on the information you provide. The more detail you give us, the more accurately it represents your business to callers.

Tested before go-live

We run through a range of call scenarios — booking requests, edge cases, out-of-hours calls, and language switches — before the agent goes live on your number.

Iterable after deployment

Call transcripts are available for review. If the agent handles something incorrectly, we update its instructions. The agent improves over time as edge cases are addressed.
