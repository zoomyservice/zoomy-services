Capabilities

What the agent handles.

01 — Voice
Natural conversation
in any language
The agent speaks with a natural, human-quality voice — not a robotic phone tree. It understands and responds conversationally, holds context throughout the call, and automatically switches language to match the caller. Configuration options include voice selection, speaking speed, and tone to match your brand.
02 — Booking
Live appointment
scheduling
The agent checks your calendar in real time during the call, offers available time slots naturally (“we have availability from 10 AM to 3 PM on Saturday — what works for you?”), collects the caller’s name and contact number, and confirms the booking directly into your calendar. Cancellations and rescheduling are handled the same way.
03 — Knowledge
Trained on your
business
The agent is built around your specific business — your services, prices, opening hours, location, policies, and FAQs. It answers questions accurately because it only knows what you’ve provided. When it doesn’t know something, it says so clearly and offers an alternative — it does not improvise or guess.
04 — Integrations
Connected to your
existing tools
The agent connects to your calendar, CRM, or other systems via webhooks. During a live call it can check availability, create records, look up existing bookings, and trigger actions in your backend — all without any manual intervention. Integration scope is defined during the project brief.
05 — Availability
24/7 — every call
answered
The agent operates at all hours, handles multiple concurrent calls, and does not take days off. Calls outside business hours are handled according to rules you define — whether that means taking a message, offering the next available slot, or providing a specific out-of-hours response.
06 — Quality
Call transcripts
& quality review
Every call generates a full transcript. You can review exactly what was said, how the agent handled each request, and where it may have responded incorrectly or incompletely. This makes it straightforward to identify gaps in the agent's knowledge and improve its responses over time. Transcripts can also be used for staff training, compliance purposes, or general call quality monitoring.
