What we deliver

Three things every campaign needs.

Great campaigns don't fail because of the platform — they fail because the creative is weak, the tracking is broken, or the setup was never properly structured. We fix all three.

01 — Campaign Files
Campaign Files & Structure
Everything you need to launch a Meta campaign — built, documented, and ready to upload or hand off to your media buyer. We define the structure, write the copy, configure the audiences, and deliver a complete setup package.
  • Campaign objective & adset structure defined
  • Audience configurations built and documented
  • Ad copy written for each placement and format
  • UTM parameter structure configured
  • Creative brief for each ad format included
  • Full setup doc — ready to upload or hand off

02 — Pixel & Tracking
Pixel & Tracking Setup
Ads are only as good as the data feeding them. We install, configure, and verify your Meta Pixel and conversion events so the platform optimises against real signals — not noise. Fresh setups or full audits of existing installs.
  • Meta Pixel installed and verified across all pages
  • Conversion events configured — purchase, lead, add-to-cart
  • Event deduplication and misfire diagnosis
  • UTM and GA4 cross-checked against pixel data
  • Test events confirmed firing before go-live
  • Full fix report with all changes documented

Pixel & Tracking

Tracking setup & audit.

Most campaigns underperform because the tracking is wrong — misfired events, duplicate pixels, no conversion data reaching the ad platform. We fix that. Whether you're starting fresh or inheriting a broken setup, we get the data flowing correctly before money is spent optimising against bad signals.

New setup
$399 / one-time

Full tracking configuration from scratch. Meta Pixel, conversion events, UTM parameters, and GA4 connection. Everything verified firing correctly before launch.

  • Meta Pixel installed and verified
  • Conversion events configured (purchase, lead, add-to-cart, etc.)
  • UTM parameter structure set up
  • GA4 linked and cross-checked
  • Test events confirmed before go-live
Existing setup audit
