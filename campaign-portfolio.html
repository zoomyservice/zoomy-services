Paid Media

Campaigns
created and managed.

We create and manage paid media campaigns across Meta, Google, TikTok, and LinkedIn. That means the initial setup, the ongoing optimisation, and the reporting — handled by us, in your ad account.

How it works

What we handle.

Campaign management is an ongoing process. Below is what we do at each stage.

Phase 01
Strategy &
Setup

Before the campaign goes live, we define the structure, configure the targeting, write the copy, and set up all the tracking. The campaign is built to a clear objective and a defined budget.

  • Campaign objective and structure based on your goal
  • Audience research and targeting configuration
  • Ad copy written for each format and placement
  • Creative brief if new assets need to be produced
  • UTM parameters and conversion tracking configured
  • Pixel and event setup verified before launch
Phase 02
Launch &
Monitoring

Once live, we monitor performance actively. Early spend is watched closely for delivery issues, audience mismatches, or anything that needs adjustment before scale.

  • Campaign reviewed and live within the agreed timeframe
  • Early performance monitored for delivery and click-through
  • Budget pacing checked against projection
  • Any technical issues with tracking resolved immediately
Phase 03
Optimisation

As the campaign collects data, we make adjustments to improve results. This includes audience refinement, pausing underperforming ads, shifting budget toward what is working, and testing new variations where relevant.

  • Adset and ad-level performance reviewed regularly
  • Budget reallocated toward lower cost-per-result
  • Underperforming ads paused and replaced
  • Audience expansion or exclusion applied where appropriate
  • Bid strategy adjusted as the campaign matures
  • New creative tested when existing ads fatigue
Phase 04
Reporting

We provide regular performance reports covering what was spent, what it produced, what changed, and what the focus is for the next period. Reports are written to be understood without needing a background in advertising.

  • Spend, impressions, clicks, and conversions
  • Cost per result tracked against benchmark
  • What changed since the last report and why
  • Recommended focus for the next period
Platforms

Where we run campaigns.

Each platform has different mechanics, audiences, and creative formats. We work with each one differently depending on what the objective requires.

Meta Ads
Facebook and Instagram campaigns covering both prospecting and retargeting. Includes custom audiences, lookalikes, dynamic product ads, and full-funnel structures. We manage the pixel, conversion events, and catalogue feeds where applicable.
Google Ads
Search, Display, Performance Max, and YouTube campaigns. We manage keyword strategy, negative lists, ad copy, bidding, and conversion tracking. Reporting is tied to actual conversion data, not just clicks.
TikTok Ads
In-Feed and TopFeed campaigns for platforms where short-form video is the dominant format. We handle audience setup, creative briefing, pixel configuration, and performance monitoring.
LinkedIn Ads
B2B campaigns using job title, seniority, company size, and industry targeting. Sponsored Content, InMail, and Lead Gen Forms. Used when the target audience is best reached by professional profile data rather than interest-based signals.
