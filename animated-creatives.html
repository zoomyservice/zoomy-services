What we make

Built for the feed. Designed to stop the scroll.

These are fully animated ads — not live-action edits, not slideshows. Motion graphics and AI-assisted animation, designed for how people consume content across Google Ads, social feeds, and display placements. No camera crew, no studio, no waiting on footage.

Format 01
Square Feed — 1:1
Facebook and Instagram feed. The default format for most campaign objectives. 1080×1080px, up to 30 seconds.
Format 02
Vertical Feed — 4:5
Taller crop for Instagram feed — takes up more screen space than square. Higher visibility in the scroll. 1080×1350px.
Format 03
Story & Reels — 9:16
Full-screen vertical for Stories and Reels placements. 1080×1920px. Required for story-placed campaigns.
